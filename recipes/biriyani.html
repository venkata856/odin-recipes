Biriyani

Biriyani is south indian spicey dish, that everyone seem too enjoy

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • herbs
  • spices
  • Ghee
  • yogurt

Steps

  1. cook rice till its 70% done
  2. martinet the chicken with spices and yogurt
  3. pour the cooked rice on the marinated chicken and seige the lid
  4. cook for 10 minutes on the medium heat
